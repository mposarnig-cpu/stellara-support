2. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "Stellara" (the "App") and explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws.

Legal bases for processing are Art. 6 GDPR — specifically performance of contract (b), your consent (a), and legitimate interests in App functionality (f).

3. Data We Process

3.1 Data You Provide

  • First name: for personalized greetings in horoscopes and affirmations.
  • Date of birth, time of birth, place of birth: to calculate your individual horoscope and astrological profile.

This data is stored on our servers (hosted by Railway, EU region) and used solely to generate your personalized content.

3.2 Purchase and Subscription Data

We use RevenueCat, Inc. (1161 Mission St, San Francisco, CA 94103, USA) to manage in-app purchases (Stellara Plus, Stellara Pro). RevenueCat creates an anonymous customer ID and stores your current subscription status. Payments are processed by Apple; we do not receive any credit card information.

More information: revenuecat.com/privacy

3.3 Technical Data / Diagnostics

In the event of an app crash, anonymized crash reports are collected (device type, iOS version, stack trace). This data is processed via Expo/EAS (Expo, Inc., USA) solely for error analysis. It is not linked to your identity.

3.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No location data (GPS)
  • No contacts, photos, or calendar access
  • No advertising or tracking IDs
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking
  • No sharing with advertising networks

4. Horoscope Generation (AI)

To generate horoscopes, affirmations, and answers to your questions, your astrological inputs (zodiac sign, date of birth, etc.) are transmitted to an AI service (Anthropic Claude API). No personal identifiers (name, user ID) beyond the astrological calculation parameters are transmitted.

More information about Anthropic's privacy practices: anthropic.com/privacy

5. Data Recipients

Your data is transmitted to the following service providers, who act as data processors on our behalf:

  • Railway Corp. (USA) — hosting of our backend server (EU data region)
  • RevenueCat, Inc. (USA) — subscription and purchase management
  • Apple Inc. — payment processing via the App Store
  • Anthropic, PBC (USA) — AI-powered horoscope generation
  • Expo, Inc. (USA) — crash reports

For transfers to the USA, EU Standard Contractual Clauses and/or Data Privacy Framework (DPF) certifications are in place.

6. Retention Period

Your profile data (name, date of birth, etc.) is stored for as long as you use the App. You can delete your data at any time in the App settings. Upon profile deletion, all associated data is removed from our servers without undue delay.

Purchase and subscription records are retained in accordance with tax law requirements (7 years under Austrian law, § 132 BAO).

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access (Art. 15)
  • Rectification (Art. 16)
  • Erasure (Art. 17)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18)
  • Data portability (Art. 20)
  • Object (Art. 21)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (dsb.gv.at)

To exercise your rights, simply email mposarnig@gmail.com.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
